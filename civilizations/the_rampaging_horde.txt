Valuable Resource: Animals

Basic Unit (cost 1):

Grunt (Strength: 1)
    Permanent: +2 strength if your hand is empty.


Shock Unit (cost 2):

Raider (Strength: 2)
    Permanent: +1 level when your hand is empty.
    Immediate: Get rid of a unit of cost 2 or less on this front.
    Delayed: If the first ability was not triggered:
             Level 1: +1 level to all Raider units on all fronts.
             Level 2: get rid of a unit of cost 2 or more on this front, chosen by the other player.
             Level 3: get rid of a unit of cost 2 or more on this front.


Main Units (cost 3):

Shield Warrior (Strength: 3)
    Immediate: Destroy a set-aside card OR discard a card from your hand.

Charge Leader (Strength: 3)
    Reaction (condition: your hand is empty): reveal the top card of your deck. If it is a unit, you may add it to this row. Otherwise discard it.

Shaman (Strength: 2)
    Immediate: Choose a type of unit. For the rest of the battle, all allied units of this type are resistant.
    Immediate: Draw 1 card, then choose 1 card or more from your hand and put each of them either on top or at the bottom of your deck in any order.

Brute (Strength: 4)
    Permanent: Resistant when your hand is not empty.

Champion Unit (cost 5):

Warlord (Strength: 5)
    Permanent: +4 strength when your hand is empty.
    Post-combat: If your hand is empty, restore this unit.


Immortal Unit (cost 6):

Far Seer (Strength: 4)
    Immediate: Reveal up to 5 cards from the bottom of your deck. You may exchange one of those with a card from your hand. Put back each revealed card either on top or at the bottom of your deck in any order.
