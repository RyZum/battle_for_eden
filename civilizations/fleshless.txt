Valuable Resource: Animals

Basic Unit (cost 1):

Skeleton (Strength: 1)
    Permanent: You may discard this card from your hand at any time to draw a card.
    Delayed: If all allied units on this front are Skeleton units, add a Skeleton units from your discard pile to the battle.


Shock Unit (cost 2):

Flameskull (Strength: 2)
    Immediate: Destroy a unit on this front of cost equal to or less than the number of units in your discard pile. You may reveal the top card of your deck, then either discard it or put it back on top of your deck.


Main Units (cost 3):

Wendigo (Strength: 3)
    Immediate: Destroy the top card of your discard pile.

Death Knight (Strength: 3)
    Immediate: Draw the non-immortal unit card that is closest to the top of your discard pile into your hand.

Skeleton Rider (Strength: 3)
    Immediate: discard up to 2 cards from your hand to draw as many.

Bone Golem (Strength: 3)
    Permanent: +1 strength for each Skeleton unit on this front.


Champion Unit (cost 5):

Lich (Strength: 3)
    Permanent: +1 strength for each two units in your discard pile.


Immortal Unit (cost 6):

Grim Reaper (Strength: 4)
    Immediate: Reorder your discard pile, then draw the top non-immortal-unit card into your hand.
