Valuable Resource: Animals

Basic Unit (cost 1):

Skeleton (Strength: 1)
    Permanent: You may discard this card from your hand at any time to draw a card.
    Delayed: If all allied units on all fronts are Skeleton units, add all Skeleton units from your discard pile to the battle.


Shock Unit (cost 2):

Flameskull (Strength: 2)
    Immediate: Get rid of a unit of cost 2 or less on this front. If there are 5 or more units in your discard pile, you may ignore the cost restriction.
    Reaction (event: this unit's first ability is triggered): Reveal the top card of your deck. You may discard it or put it back on top of your deck.


Main Units (cost 3):

Wendigo (Strength: 3)
    Immediate: Destroy the top card of your discard pile.

Death Knight (Strength: 3)
    Immediate: Draw the non-immortal unit card that is closest to the top of your discard pile into your hand.

Skeleton Rider (Strength: 3)
    Immediate: Draw 1 card, then discard 1 card from your hand.

Bone Golem (Strength: 3)
    Permanent: +2 strength for each Skeleton unit on this front.


Champion Unit (cost 5):

Lich (Strength: 3)
    Permanent: +1 strength for each unit in your discard pile.


Immortal Unit (cost 6):

Grim Reaper (Strength: 4)
    Immediate: Reorder your discard pile, then draw the top non-Grim-Reaper card into your hand.
