Basic Unit (cost 1):

Skeleton (Strength: 1)
    Permanent: You may discard this card from your hand at any time to draw a card.
    Delayed: If all allied units on all fronts are Skeleton units, add all Skeleton units from your discard pile to the battle.


Shock Unit (cost 2):

Flameskull (Strength: 2)
    Immediate: [If the units in your discard pile number 3 or more: get rid of a unit of cost 2 or less on this front. You may ignore the cost restriction if they number 7 or more] OR [Reveal the top card of your deck and either discard it or put it back on top of your deck].
    Reaction (event: tihis unit's first ability is triggered): Reveal the top card of your deck. You may discard it or put it back on top of your deck.


Main Units (cost 3):

Wendigo (Strength: 2)
    Immediate: Destroy the top card of your discard pile.

Death Knight (Strength: 2)
    Immediate: [Reveal the top card of your deck, then either discard it or put it back on top of your deck] OR [draw the non-Grim-Reaper unit card that is closest to the top of your discard pile into your hand].

Skeleton Rider (Strength: 2)
    Immediate: Draw 1 card, then discard 1 card from your hand.

Bone Golem (Strength: 3)
    Permanent: +2 strength for each Skeleton unit on this front.


Champion Unit (cost 5):

Lich (Strength: 3)
    Permanent: +1 strength for each unit in your discard pile.


Immortal Unit (cost 6):

Grim Reaper (Strength: 3)
    Immediate: Choose a type of units. Move all units of that type that are in your discard pile to the bottom of it. Draw the non-Grim Reaper unit card that is closest to the top of your discard pile into your hand.
