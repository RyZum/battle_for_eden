Valuable Resource: Ore

Basic Unit (cost 1):

Infantry Squad (Strength: 1)
    Immediate: Reveal the top 2 cards of your deck. You may discard the first card. If it is a unit of strength 1, you may add it to the battle on this front. Put back the remaining card(s) on top of your deck in any order.


Shock Unit (cost 2):

Archery Squad (Strength: 1)
    Immediate: Discard a unit of strength 1 from your hand to get rid of a unit on this front. This ability cannot be triggered when this unit is added to the battle by another unit.


Main Units (cost 3):

Tactician (Strength: 1)
    Permanent: +1 strength if there is at least 1 Infantry Squad and 1 Archery Squad on this front.
    Immediate: Reveal the top card of your deck, then discard it, destroy it or put it back on top of your deck.

Heavy Infantry (Strength: 1)
    Permanent: +2 strength in battle.

Captain (Strength: 1)
    Immediate: Reveal the top 3 cards of your deck. Discard any number of those cards and put the rest on top of your deck in any order. If units of strength 1 were revealed in this way, you may draw up to 2 into your hand. If you draw 2, immediately add a unit from your hand to the battle on this front.

Cavalry (Strength: 1)
    Immediate: Discard a unit of strength 1 from your hand to draw 3 cards, then discard 2 cards from your hand.
    Permanent: +1 strength if the first ability was triggered.


Champion Unit (cost 5):

Knight (Strength: 2)
    Permanent: +1 strength to all Infantry Squads and Archery Squads on this front.


Immortal Unit (cost 6):

General (Strength: 2)
    Immediate: Trigger the immediate ability of an allied unit on this front.
    Delayed: Discard a unit of strength 1 from your hand to add 4 strength to this unit.
