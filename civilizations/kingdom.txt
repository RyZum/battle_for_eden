Valuable Resource: Ore

Basic Unit (cost 1):

Infantry (Strength: 1)
    Immediate: Reveal the top card of your deck. If it is a unit of strength 1, add it to the battle on this front. Otherwise discard it.


Shock Unit (cost 2):

Spearman (Strength: 1)
    Immediate: Discard a unit of strength 1 from your hand to destroy an enemy unit.


Warrior Unit (cost 3):

Recruiter (Strength: 1)
    Immediate: Reveal the top two cards from your deck. Draw into your hand all units of strength 1 that are not Recruiter units. Discard any number of remaining cards and put the rest back on top of the deck in any order. OR Destroy 1 card from your hand.


Tactical Unit (cost 3):

Cavalry (Strength: 1)
    Immediate: Discard a unit of strength 1 from your hand to draw 2 cards, then discard 1 card from your hand.


Champion Unit (cost 5):

Knight (Strength: 2)
    Permanent: +1 strength for each type of allied unit on this front


Immortal Unit (cost 6):

General (Strength: 5)
    Immediate: Trigger an allied unit's immediate ability.
