Valuable Resource: Ore

Basic Unit (cost 1):

Scout (Strength: 1)
    Immediate: Reveal the top card of your deck. You may discard it or put it back on top of your deck. You may add a unit of strength 1 revealed in this way to the battle on this front.


Shock Unit (cost 2):

Spearman (Strength: 1)
    Immediate: Discard a unit of strength 1 from your hand to destroy a unit on this front. This ability cannot be triggered when this unit is added to the battle by another unit.


Main Units (cost 3):

Tactician (Strength: 1)
    Immediate: Destroy a card from your hand.

Heavy Infantry (Strength: 1)
    Permanent: +2 Strength in battle.

Captain (Strength: 1)
    Immediate: Reveal the top 2 cards of your deck. Discard any number of those cards and put the rest on top of your deck in any order. You may also choose to draw non-Captain units of strength 1 into your hand.

Cavalry (Strength: 1)
    Immediate: Discard a unit of strength 1 from your hand to draw 2 cards, then discard 1 card.


Champion Unit (cost 5):

Knight (Strength: 2)
    Permanent: +1 strength for each two allied units on this front.


Immortal Unit (cost 6):

General (Strength: 2)
    Immediate: Trigger the immediate ability of an allied unit on this front.
    Delayed: Discard a unit of strength 1 from your hand to add 4 strength to this unit.
