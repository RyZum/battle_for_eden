Valuable Resource: Ore

Basic Unit (cost 1):

Soldier (Strength: 1)
    Immediate: Reveal the top 2 cards of your deck. Discard any number of those cards and put the rest on top of your deck in any order. You may also choose to add 1 unit of strength 1 revealed in this way to the battle on this front.


Shock Unit (cost 2):

Spearman (Strength: 1)
    Immediate: Discard a unit of strength 1 from your hand to get rid of a unit on this front. This ability cannot be triggered when this unit is added to the battle by another unit.


Main Units (cost 3):

Tactician (Strength: 1)
    Permanent: +1 strength if there is at least 1 Soldier on this front.
    Permanent: +1 strength if there is at least 1 Spearman on this front.
    Immediate: Reveal the top card of your deck, then discard it, destroy it or put it back on top of your deck.

Heavy Infantry (Strength: 1)
    Permanent: +3 Strength in battle.

Captain (Strength: 1)
    Immediate: Reveal the top 3 cards of your deck. Discard any number of those cards and put the rest on top of your deck in any order. You may also choose to draw up to 2 non-Captain units of strength 1 into your hand.

Cavalry (Strength: 1)
    Immediate: Discard a unit of strength 1 from your hand to draw 3 cards, then discard 2 card.
    Permanent: +1 strength if the first ability was triggered.


Champion Unit (cost 5):

Knight (Strength: 2)
    Permanent: +1 strength to all Soldier and Spearman units.


Immortal Unit (cost 6):

General (Strength: 2)
    Immediate: Trigger the immediate ability of an allied unit on this front.
    Delayed: Discard a unit of strength 1 from your hand to add 4 strength to this unit.
