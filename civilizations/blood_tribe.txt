Valuable Resource: Plants

Basic Unit (cost 1):

Tribal Warrior (Strength: 1)
    Permanent: +1 strength when doomed.
    Immediate: Doom this unit.


Shock Unit (cost 2):

Berserker (Strength: 2)
    Reaction (doomed or destroyed): Destroy or doom a unit on this front.


Main Units (cost 3):

Tribe Elder (Strength: 3)
    Immediate: Doom an allied a unit on this front to destroy a card from your hand.

Bloodsmith (Strength: 3)
    Immediate: Doom an allied unit on this front to draw a card.

Herbalist (Strength: 3)
    Aftermath: Save a doomed unit on this front.

Blood Lord (Strength: 3)
    Permanent: +2 for each doomed allied unit on this front.


Champion Unit (cost 5):

Poison Master (Strength: 4)
    Immediate: Doom any number of allied unit on this front, save any number of other allied units on this front.


Immortal Unit (cost 6):

Blood Alchemist (Strength: 3)
    Delayed: Save or destroy a doomed unit on this front to absorb its strength (max total strength: 8).
