Valuable Resource: Plants

Basic Unit (cost 1):

Sacrificed (Strength: 1)
    Permanent: +1 strength when doomed.
    Permanent: +1 strength when it's the only allied unit on this front.


Shock Unit (cost 2):

Berserker (Strength: 2)
    Immediate: Doom an allied unit.
    Reaction (doomed): Destroy or doom an enemy unit.


Warrior Unit (cost 3):

Bloodsmith (Strength: 3)
    Immediate: Doom an allied unit to draw a card.


Tactical Unit (cost 3):

Herbalist (Strength: 3)
    Aftermath: Discard a doomed unit OR destroy a card from your hand (there must be an opponent to choose this).


Champion Unit (cost 5):

Poison Master (Strength: 4)
    Immediate: Save all doomed allied units and doom all others.


Immortal Unit (cost 6):

Blood Alchemist (Strength: 4)
    Delayed: Save or destroy a doomed unit to absorb its strength.
