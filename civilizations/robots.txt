Valuable Resource: Positronium

Basic Unit (cost 1):

Building Block (Strength: 0)
    Immediate: Destroy 2 Building Blocks on this front to add a Weaponry Module from your World to the battle on this front.
    Delayed: If there is no other unit on this front: doom this unit add 2 to its strength.


Shock Unit (cost 2):

Weaponry Module (Strength: 2)
    Immediate: Destroy a Building Block on this front to get rid of a unit on this front.
    Immediate: Destroy this unit to add a combination of units of same total cost from your World to the battle on this front. Do not trigger their immediate abilities.


Main Units (cost 3):

Garbage Disposal Module (Strength: 3)
    Immediate: Destroy a Building Block on this front to destroy a card from your hand.
    Immediate: Destroy this unit to add a combination of units of same total cost from your World to the battle on this front. Do not trigger their immediate abilities.

Production Module (Strength: 3)
    Immediate: Add all Building Blocks from your World to the battle on this front. Do not trigger their immediate abilities.
    Immediate: Destroy this unit to add a combination of units of same total cost from your World to the battle on this front. Do not trigger their immediate abilities.

Flight Module (Strength: 3)
    Immediate: Move any number of Building Blocks from and to any number of active fronts.
    Immediate: Destroy a Building Block on this front to draw a card.
    Immediate: Destroy this unit to add a combination of units of same total cost from your World to the battle on this front. Do not trigger their immediate abilities.

Efficiency Module (Strength: 3)
    Delayed: Destroy any number of Building Blocks on this front.
    Immediate: Destroy this unit to add a combination of units of same total cost from your World to the battle on this front. Do not trigger their immediate abilities.


Champion Unit (cost 5):

Core Module (Strength: 1)
    Immediate: Destroy 2 Building Blocks.
    Immediate: Destroy up to 2 Building Blocks to move as many allied units on this front to the side of this unit.
    Permanent: +2 strength to the units moved by the second ability. If the first ability was triggered: +2 strength to the units above and below this unit.


Immortal Unit (cost 6):

Assembly Module (Strength: 3)
    Immediate: Destroy up to 3 Building Blocks on this front. You may add a unit of cost equal to or less than the number of units destroyed in this way to the battle on this front. Doom that unit.
