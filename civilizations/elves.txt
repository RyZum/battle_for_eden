Valuable Resource: Plants

Basic Unit (cost 1):

Hunter (Strength: 1)
    Immediate: Generate as many mana tokens on this front as this territory could produce plants and animals.
    Delayed: Spend 2 mana on this front to add 1 strength to this unit.


Shock Unit (cost 2):

Magic Archer (Strength: 2)
    Permanent: If this unit should be wounded while undoomed, doom it instead.
    Immediate: Generate as many mana tokens on this front as this territory could produce plants and animals.
    Reaction (mana production on this front): Spend 5 mana on this front to destroy a unit on this front. If this unit is doomed, destroy it.


Main Units (cost 3):

Mystic (Strength: 3)
    Permanent: If this unit should be wounded while undoomed, doom it instead.
    Immediate: Generate as many mana tokens on this front as this territory could produce plants and animals.
    Reaction (mana production on this front): Spend 5 mana on this front to destroy a card from your hand. If this unit is doomed, destroy it.
    
Druid (Strength: 3)
    Permanent: If this unit should be wounded while undoomed, doom it instead.
    Immediate: Generate as many mana tokens on this front as this territory could produce plants and animals.
    Reaction (mana production on this front): Spend 5 mana on this front to draw a card or make the opponent choose and discard a card from his hand. If this unit is doomed, destroy it.

Channeler (Strength: 3)
    Immediate: Generate as many mana tokens on this front as both this territory and the neighbouring ones could produce plants or animals (choose for each territory).

Sorcerer (Strength: 3)
    Immediate: Generate as many mana tokens on this front as this territory could produce plants and animals.
    Delayed: Spend 2 mana on this front to add 2 strength to this unit.


Champion Unit (cost 5):

Grand Channeler (Strength: 3)
    Immediate: Generate as many mana tokens on this front as both this territory and the neighbouring ones could produce plants and animals.


Immortal Unit (cost 6):

Spirit of Nature (Strength: 4)
    Permanent: If this unit should be destroyed while it is undoomed, doom it instead.
    Immediate: Generate as many mana tokens on this front as this territory could produce plants and animals.
    Reaction (mana production on this front): generate 1 mana on this front.
