Valuable Resource: Plants

Basic Unit (cost 1):

Hunter (Strength: 1)
    Immediate: Produce as many mana on this front as the sum of the allied territory's production capacities in plants and animals.
    Delayed: Spend 2 mana on this front to add 1 strength to this unit.


Shock Unit (cost 2):

Magic Archer (Strength: 2)
    Permanent: If this unit should collapse while undoomed, doom it instead. It then collapses the next time its reaction ability is triggered.
    Immediate: Produce as many mana on this front as the sum of the allied territory's production capacities in plants and animals.
    Reaction (event: mana production on this front): Spend 5 mana on this front to get rid of a unit on this front.


Main Units (cost 3):

Druid (Strength: 3)
    Permanent: If this unit should collapse while undoomed, doom it instead. It then collapses the next time its reaction ability is triggered.
    Immediate: Produce as many mana on this front as the sum of the allied territory's production capacities in plants and animals.
    Reaction (event: mana production on this front): Spend 4 mana on this front to destroy a card from your hand.
    
Ranger (Strength: 3)
    Permanent: If this unit should collapse while undoomed, doom it instead. It then collapses the next time its reaction ability is triggered.
    Immediate: Produce as many mana on this front as the sum of the allied territory's production capacities in plants and animals.
    Reaction (event: mana production on this front): Spend 4 mana on this front to draw a card or make your opponent choose and discard one from his hand.

Channeler (Strength: 3)
    Immediate: For the allied territory and each of the neighbouring ones: add as many mana on this front as this territory's production capacity in plants or animals.

Mystic (Strength: 3)
    Immediate: Produce twice as many mana on this front as the sum of the allied territory's production capacities in plants and animals.
    Immediate: Spend 2 mana on this front to draw a card then discard a card.


Champion Unit (cost 5):

Sorcerer (Strength: 4)
    Immediate: Produce as many mana on this front as the sum of the allied territory's production capacities in plants and animals.
    Delayed: Spend up to 3 mana on this front to add 2 strength to this unit for each mana spent this way.


Immortal Unit (cost 6):

Spirit of Nature (Strength: 4)
    Permanent: If this unit should collapse while undoomed, doom it instead.
    Permanent: All mana productions on this front are increased by 1 mana.
    Immediate: Produce as many mana on this front as the sum of the allied territory's production capacities in plants and animals.
