Valuable Resource: Plants

Basic Unit (cost 1):

Mystic (Strength: 1)
    Immediate: Generate as many natural mana tokens on this front as this territory could produce plants, animals and mana shards (max: 5).
    Delayed: Spend 2 natural mana on this front to add 1 strength to this unit.


Shock Unit (cost 2):

Magic Archer (Strength: 2)
    Immediate: Spend 5 natural mana tokens from this front to either destroy 1 unit (allied or enemy) OR force your opponent to choose and discard 1 card from his hand.


Warrior Unit (cost 3):

Druid (Strength: 3)
    Immediate: Spend 5 natural mana tokens from this front to draw 1 card.


Tactical Unit (cost 3):

Channeler (Strength: 3)
    Immediate: Generate as many natural mana tokens on this front as both this territory and the neighbouring ones could produce plants, animals or mana shards (choose for each territory) OR destroy 1 Mana Crystal card from your hand to generate 3 natural mana tokens on this front.


Champion Unit (cost 5):

Sorcerer (Strength: 4)
    Delayed: Spend 5 natural mana tokens from this front to add 3 strength to this unit.


Immortal Unit (cost 6):

Spirit of Nature (Strength: 1)
    Permanent: If this unit should be destroyed while it is undoomed, doom it instead.
    Permanent: +3 strength for each natural mana token placed on this card (max: +6 strength).
    Reaction (natural mana tokens added to this territory): move a natural mana token from this front to this card.
