Valuable Resource: Plants

Basic Unit (cost 1):

Hunter (Strength: 1)
    Immediate: Generate as many mana tokens on this front as this territory could produce plants and animals.
    Delayed: Spend 2 mana on this front to add 1 strength to this unit.


Shock Unit (cost 2):

Magic Archer (Strength: 2)
    Permanent: If this unit should be destroyed while undoomed, doom it instead.
    Immediate: Generate as many mana tokens on this front as this territory could produce plants and animals.
    Reaction (mana production on this front): Spend 4 mana on this front to destroy a unit on this front. If this Magic Archer unit is doomed, destroy it.


Main Units (cost 3):

Druid (Strength: 3)
    Permanent: If this unit should be destroyed while undoomed, doom it instead.
    Immediate: Generate as many mana tokens on this front as this territory could produce plants and animals.
    Reaction (mana production on this front): Spend 4 mana on this front to destroy a card from your hand. If this Druid unit is doomed, destroy it.
    
Ranger (Strength: 3)
    Permanent: If this unit should be destroyed while undoomed, doom it instead.
    Immediate: Generate as many mana tokens on this front as this territory could produce plants and animals.
    Reaction (mana production on this front): Spend 4 mana on this front to draw a card or make the opponent choose and discard a card from his hand. If this Ranger unit is doomed, destroy it.

Channeler (Strength: 3)
    Immediate: Generate as many mana tokens on this front as both this territory and the neighbouring ones could produce plants or animals (choose for each territory).

Mystic (Strength: 3)
    Immediate: Generate twice as many mana tokens on this front as this territory could produce plants and animals, and 1 more for each neighbouring territory that could produce more than 1 animal or 1 plant token.
    Immediate: Spend 2 mana on this front to draw a card then discard a card.


Champion Unit (cost 5):

Sorcerer (Strength: 4)
    Immediate: Generate as many mana tokens on this front as this territory could produce plants and animals.
    Delayed: Spend up to 3 mana on this front to add as much strength to this unit.


Immortal Unit (cost 6):

Spirit of Nature (Strength: 4)
    Permanent: If this unit should be destroyed while it is undoomed, doom it instead.
    Immediate: Generate as many mana tokens on this front as this territory could produce plants and animals.
    Reaction (mana production on this front): generate 1 mana on this front.
