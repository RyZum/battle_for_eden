Valuable Resource: Animals

Basic Unit (cost 1):

Skeleton (Strength: 1)
    Immediate: Discard the top card of your deck OR add a Skeleton from your discard pile to the battle on this front.


Shock Unit (cost 2):

Wendigo (Strength: 2)
    Immediate: Destroy an enemy unit of cost equal to or less than the number of units in your discard pile.


Warrior Unit (cost 3):

Death Knight (Strength: 3)
    Immediate: Draw the non-immortal unit card that is closest to the top of your discard pile into your hand.


Tactical Unit (cost 3):

Mummy (Strength: 3)
    Immediate: Discard the top card of your deck and repeat the operation up to 3 more times OR destroy the top card of your discard pile. 
    Reaction (enemy unit destroyed): discard the top card of your deck.


Champion Unit (cost 5):

Lich (Strength: 3)
    Permanent: +1 strength to each allied unit on the same front.


Immortal Unit (cost 6):

Dark Lord (Strength: 4)
    Immediate: Reorder your discard pile, then draw the top card into your hand. Immortal units cannot be drawn in this way.
