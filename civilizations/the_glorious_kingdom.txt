Civilization Resource: Ore

Basic Unit (cost 1):

Infantry Squad (Strength: 1)
    Permanent: Discard this unit when a unit on its row collapses or is discarded.
    Immediate: If there are 2 Infantry Squad units or less on this row: reveal cards from the top of your deck until you reveal a unit of strength 1 or there is no more card to reveal. You may add this unit to the row. Put back the revealed cards on top of your deck in the order in which they were revealed.

Shock Unit (cost 2):

Archery Squad (Strength: 1)
    Permanent: When you purchase this card, you may purchase 1 Infantry Squad unit card for free.
    Immediate: Discard a unit of strength 1 from your hand to draw 1 card and get rid of a unit on this front.


Main Units (cost 3):

Tactician (Strength: 1)
    Permanent: When you purchase this card, you may purchase 1 Infantry Squad unit card for free.
    Permanent: Whenever this unit is revealed by an ability, you may destroy one of the other revealed cards.

Heavy Infantry (Strength: 1)
    Permanent: When you purchase this card, you may purchase 1 Infantry Squad unit card for free.
    Permanent: +2 strength if there is at least 1 other unit on the row.

Captain (Strength: 1)
    Permanent: When you purchase this card, you may purchase 1 Infantry Squad unit card for free.
    Immediate: Discard a unit of strength 1 from your hand to draw 1 card and copy the immediate ability of an allied unit of strength 1 on this front. When copying an Infantry Squad's ability, units of strength 2 can be added to the row as if their strength was 1.

Cavalry (Strength: 1)
    Permanent: When you purchase this card, you may purchase 1 Infantry Squad unit card for free.
    Immediate: Discard a unit of strength 1 from your hand to draw 3 cards, then discard 2 cards from your hand.
    Permanent: +1 strength if the immediate ability was triggered.


Champion Unit (cost 5):

Knight (Strength: 2)
    Permanent: +1 strength to all Infantry Squads and Archery Squads on this front.


Immortal Unit (cost 6):

General (Strength: 2)
    Immediate: Draw 1 card.
