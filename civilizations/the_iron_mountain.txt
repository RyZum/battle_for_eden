Valuable Resource: Ore

Basic Unit (cost 1):

Miner (Strength: 1)
    Immediate: Harvest ore from the allied territory and 2 neighbouring territories.
    Immediate: Reveal the top 2 cards of your deck. You may draw 1 revealed Energy Crystal card into your hand. Discard the rest.


Shock Unit (cost 2):

Artificer (Strength: 1)
    Immediate: Use 3 Energy Crystals to draw a card and harvest ore from the allied territory and 1 neighbouring territory.
    Immediate: If you triggered the first ability: use 5 ore on the allied territory to get rid of a unit on this front. If that unit's cost is 2 or less, you may use only 3 ore instead.


Main Units (cost 3):

Rushed Cannon (Strength: 2)
    Permanent: You may purchase this unit at any moment. When you do, put it in your hand instead of your discard pile.
    Immediate: Discard an Energy Crystal card from your hand then draw a card.
    Post-combat: Destroy this unit.

Digging Machine (Strength: 2)
    Immediate: Harvest ore from the allied territory and all neighbouring territories.
    Immediate: Reveal the top 2 cards of your deck. Discard any number of those cards and put the rest on top of your deck in any order. You may also choose to draw one revealed Energy Crystal card into your hand. 

Sentinel (Strength: 1)
    Immediate: Discard one card to draw one card and harvest ore from the allied territory.
    Permanent: +1, +2, +3 or +4 strength if 3 territories that you control each have at least 5, 10, 30 or 50 ore respectively on them.

Specialist (Strength: 1)
    Immediate: Discard an Energy Crystal card to put 5 new shard tokens on the allied territory.


Champion Unit (cost 5):

Blacksmith (Strength: 1)
    Immediate: Use 3 Energy Crystals to draw a card and harvest ore from the allied territory.
    Delayed: If you triggered the first ability: for each allied unit on this front, you may use 1 ore from the allied territory to add 1 strength to that unit.


Immortal Unit (cost 6):

Rocket Roll (Strength: 3)
    Immediate: [Move any number of resources between two territories you control] OR [destroy a card from your discard pile to get the right to immediately purchase a unit from your World and add it to your hand].
