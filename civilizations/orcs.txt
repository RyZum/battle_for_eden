Valuable Resource: Animals

Basic Unit (cost 1):

Grunt (Strength: 1)
    Permanent: +1 strength when your hand is empty.


Shock Unit (cost 2):

Raider (Strength: 2)
    Delayed: If your hand is empty, you or the opponent chooses and destroys one of his own units on this front.
    OR
    Combined delayed (2): If your hand is empty, destroy a unit on this front.


Main Units (cost 3):

Shield Warrior (Strength: 3)
    Immediate: Destroy a card from your hand.

Charge Leader (Strength: 3)
    Reaction (hand is empty): reveal the top card of your deck. If it is a unit, you can add it to the battle on this front. Otherwise discard it.

Shaman (Strength: 3)
    Permanent: Undoomed allied unit are doomed instead of destroyed.
    Immediate: Look at the top card of your deck. You may discard it or exchange it with a card of your hand. Discard any number of cards from your hand.

Troublemaker (Strength: 3)
    Immediate: Discard a card from your hand. The opponent discards a card from his hand.

Champion Unit (cost 5):

Warlord (Strength: 5)
    Permanent: +4 strength when your hand is empty.


Immortal Unit (cost 6):

Far Seer (Strength: 4)
    Immediate: Draw as many cards as you have in your hand, then put as many from your hand to the top of your deck or your discard pile.
