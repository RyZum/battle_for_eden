Valuable Resource: Ore

Basic Unit (cost 1):

Miner (Strength: 1)
    Immediate: Harvest the allied territory's ore.
    Immediate: Reveal the top 2 cards of your deck. You may draw 1 revealed Energy Crystal card into your hand. Discard the rest.


Shock Unit (cost 2):

Artificer (Strength: 2)
    Immediate: [Use 3 Energy Crystals then draw a card] to harvest the allied territory's ore.
    Immediate: If you triggered the first ability: use 5 ore from on the allied territory to get rid of a unit on this front. If that unit's cost is 2 or less, you may use only 2 ore instead.


Main Units (cost 3):

Rushed Cannon (Strength: 2)
    Permanent: You may purchase this unit at any moment. When you do, put this unit in your hand instead of your discard pile.
    Immediate: Discard an Energy Crystal card from your hand then draw a card.
    Post-combat: Get rid of this unit.

Digging Machine (Strength: 2)
    Immediate: Harvest the allied territory's ore and the neighbouring territories' ore.
    Immediate: Reveal the top 2 cards of your deck. Discard any number of those cards and put the rest on top of your deck in any order. You may also choose to draw one revealed Energy Crystal card into your hand.

Sentinel (Strength: 2)
    Immediate: [Use 3 Energy Crystals then draw a card] to harvest the allied territory's ore.
    Permanent: +1 strength if you triggered the first ability and there are 2 or more ore tokens on each of your territories.
    Permanent: +2 strength if you triggered the first ability and there are 5 or more ore tokens on each of your territories.

Specialist (Strength: 2)
    Immediate: [Discard an Energy Crystal card from your hand then draw a card] to put 2 new Shard tokens on the allied territory.


Champion Unit (cost 5):

Rocket Roll (Strength: 3)
    Immediate: Move any number of resources between the allied territory and other territories you control OR purchase a unit from your World and add it to your hand.


Immortal Unit (cost 6):

Blacksmith (Strength: 2)
    Immediate: [Use 3 Energy Crystals then draw a card] OR [Destroy an Energy Crystal card from your discard pile] to harvest the allied territory's ore.
    Delayed: If you triggered the first ability: for each allied unit on this front, use up to 2 ore from the allied territory to add as much strength to this unit.
