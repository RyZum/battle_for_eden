Valuable Resource: Ore

Basic Unit (cost 1):

Miner (Strength: 1)
    Immediate: Produce ore on this territory.
    Immediate: Reveal the top 2 cards of your deck. You may draw 1 revealed Mana Crystal card into your hand. Discard the remaining cards.


Shock Unit (cost 2):

Artificer (Strength: 2)
    Immediate: Discard a Mana Crystal card from your hand then draw a card to produce ore on this territory.
    Immediate: If you triggered the first ability, you may remove any number of ore tokens from this territory to destroy a unit on this front whose cost is equal to the number of removed tokens.


Main Units (cost 3):

Rushed Cannon (Strength: 2)
    You may purchase this unit at any moment. When you do, it goes into your hand instead of your discard pile.
    Immediate: Discard a Mana Crystal card from your hand then draw a card to produce ore on this territory.
    Aftermath: You may destroy this unit.

Digging Machine (Strength: 2)
    Immediate: Produce ore on this territory. Neighbouring territories also produce ore on this territory. 
    Immediate: Reveal the top 2 cards of your deck. Discard any number of those cards and put the rest on top of your deck in any order. If Mana Crystal cards were revealed in this way, you may draw 1 into your hand.

Sentinel (Strength: 2)
    Immediate: Discard a Mana Crystal card from your hand then draw a card to produce ore on this territory.
    Permanent: +1 strength if you triggered the first ability and there are 2 or more ore tokens on each of your territories.
    Permanent: +2 strength if you triggered the first ability and there are 5 or more ore tokens on each of your territories.

Specialist (Strength: 2)
    Immediate: Discard a Mana Crystal card from your hand then draw a card to produce a shard on this territory.


Champion Unit (cost 5):

Rocket Roll (Strength: 3)
    Immediate: Move any number of resources from this territory to other territories you control OR purchase a unit from your World and add it to your hand.


Immortal Unit (cost 6):

Blacksmith (Strength: 2)
    Immediate: [Discard a Mana Crystal card from your hand then draw a card] OR [Destroy a Mana Crystal card from your discard pile].
    Delayed: If you triggered the first ability, for each allied unit you may choose to remove 1 ore token from this territory to add 1 to that unit's strength.
