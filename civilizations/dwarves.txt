Valuable Resource: Ore

Basic Unit (cost 1):

Miner (Strength: 1)
    Immediate: Harvest the allied territory's ore.
    Immediate: Reveal the top 2 cards of your deck. You may draw 1 revealed Energy Crystal card into your hand. Discard the rest.


Shock Unit (cost 2):

Artificer (Strength: 1)
    Immediate: [Use 3 Energy Crystals then draw a card] to harvest the allied territory's ore.
    Immediate: If you triggered the first ability: use 5 ore on the allied territory to get rid of a unit on this front. If that unit's cost is 2 or less, you may use only 2 ore instead.


Main Units (cost 3):

Rushed Cannon (Strength: 2)
    Permanent: You may purchase this unit at any moment. When you do, put it in your hand instead of your discard pile.
    Immediate: Discard an Energy Crystal card from your hand then draw a card.
    Post-combat: Destroy this unit.

Digging Machine (Strength: 2)
    Immediate: Harvest the allied territory's ore and the neighbouring territories' ore.
    Immediate: Reveal the top 2 cards of your deck. Discard any number of those cards and put the rest on top of your deck in any order. Draw the Energy Crystal card which is the closest to the top of your discard pile into your hand.

Sentinel (Strength: 1)
    Immediate: Discard one card to draw one card.
    Permanent: +1, +2, +3 or +4 strength if 3 territories that you control each have at least 5, 10, 30 or 50 ore respectively on them.

Specialist (Strength: 1)
    Immediate: Put 5 new shard tokens on the allied territory.


Champion Unit (cost 5):

Blacksmith (Strength: 1)
    Immediate: [Use 3 Energy Crystals then draw a card] to harvest the allied territory's ore.
    Delayed: If you triggered the first ability: for each allied unit on this front, use up to 2 ore from the allied territory to add as much strength to that unit.


Immortal Unit (cost 6):

Rocket Roll (Strength: 3)
    Immediate: [Move any number of resources between two territories you control] OR [destroy a card from your discard pile to purchase a unit from your World and add it to your hand].
