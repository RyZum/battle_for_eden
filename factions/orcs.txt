Valuable Resource: Animals

Basic Unit (cost 1):

Grunt (Strength: 1)
    Permanent: +1 strength when your hand is empty.


Shock Unit (cost 2):

Raider (Strength: 2)
    Delayed: If your hand is empty, the opponent must choose and destroy one of his units OR if your hand is empty, change another raider's ability to "Delayed: destroy an enemy unit".


Warrior Unit (cost 3):

Charge Leader (Strength: 3)
    Permanent: When an undoomed allied unit should be destroyed, you may doom it instead.
    Reaction (hand becoming empty): reveal the top card of your deck. If it is a unit, you can add it to the battle on this front. Otherwise discard it.


Tactical Unit (cost 3):

Shaman (Strength: 3)
    Immediate: Discard one card from your hand OR destroy one card from you hand (there must be an opponent to choose this). If you discarded a card, you may draw one card.


Champion Unit (cost 5):

Far Seer (Strength: 4)
    Permanent: When an undoomed allied unit should be destroyed, you may destroy another undoomed allied unit instead.
    Immediate: Discard any number of cards, then draw up to that number of cards.


Immortal Unit (cost 6):

Warlord (Strength: 4)
    Permanent: +3 strength when your hand is empty.
