Basic Unit (cost 1):

Grunt (Strength: 1)
    Permanent: +1 strength when your hand is empty.


Shock Unit (cost 2):

Raider (Strength: 2)
    Delayed: If your hand is empty, the opponent must choose and destroy one of his units.


Warrior Unit (cost 3):

Charge Leader (Strength: 3)
    Reaction (hand becoming empty), reveal the top card of your deck. If it is a unit, you can add it to the battle on this front.


Tactical Unit (cost 3):

Shaman (Strength: 3)
    Immediate: Discard one card from your hand to draw one card OR destroy one card from you hand (there must be an opponent to choose this).


Champion Unit (cost 5):

Far Seer (Strength: 4)
    Immediate: Discard any number of cards, then draw as many.


Immortal Unit (cost 6):

Warlord (Strength: 4)
    Permanent: +3 strength when your hand is empty.
