Basic Unit (cost 1):

Skeleton (Strength: 1)
    Immediate: Discard the top card of your deck OR draw a Skeleton from your discard pile to your hand.


Warrior Unit (cost 2):

Zombie (Strength: 2)
    Immediate: Destroy an enemy unit of strength equal to or less than half the number of units in your discard pile (rounded down).


Greater Warrior Unit (cost 3):

Lich (Strength: 3)
    Immediate: Draw the non-immortal unit card that is closest to the top of your discard pile into your hand. 


Tactical Unit (cost 3):

Mummy (Strength: 3)
    Immediate: Destroy the top card of your discard pile OR put the top 4 cards of your deck in your discard pile.


Champion Unit (cost 5):

Death Knight (Strength: 3)
    Permanent: +1 strength for each allied unit on the same front.

Immortal Unit (cost 6):

Elder Lich (Strength: 4)
    Immediate: Reorder your discard pile, then draw the top card into your hand. Immortal units cannot be drawn in this way.
