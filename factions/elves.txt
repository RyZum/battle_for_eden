Basic Unit (cost 1):

Mystic (Strength: 1)
    Immediate: Place as many mana tokens on this territory as it could produce meat and plants (mana tokens are discarded after the battle).


Shock Unit (cost 2):

Magic Archer (Strength: 2)
    Immediate: Remove 5 mana tokens from this territory to destroy 1 unit (allied or enemy) OR remove 5 mana tokens from this territory to have the opponent choose and discard 1 card from his hand.


Warrior Unit (cost 3):

Druid (Strength: 3)
    Immediate: Remove 5 mana tokens from this territory to draw 1 card.


Tactical Unit (cost 3):

Channeler (Strength: 3)
    Immediate: Place as many mana tokens on this territory as both it and the neighbouring territories could produce meat and plants OR destroy a Crystal card from your hand to place 5 mana tokens on this territory (mana tokens are discarded after the battle).


Champion Unit (cost 5):

Sorcerer (Strength: 4)
    Delayed: Remove 5 mana tokens from this territory to add 3 strength to this unit.


Immortal Unit (cost 6):

Spirit of Nature (Strength: 1)
    Permanent: If this unit should be destroyed while it is undoomed, doom it instead.
    Permanent: +3 strength for each mana token placed on this card.
    Reaction (mana tokens added to this territory): remove a mana token from this territory and place it on this card.
