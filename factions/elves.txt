Basic Unit (cost 1):

Mystic (Strength: 1)
    Immediate: Place as many mana tokens on this territory as the number of meat and plants tokens it could produce. (mana tokens are discarded after the battle)


Shock Unit (cost 2):

Magic Archer (Strength: 2)
    Immediate: Remove 5 mana tokens from this territory to destroy 1 unit (allied or enemy) or have the opponent choose and discard a card from his hand.


Warrior Unit (cost 3):

Druid (Strength: 3)
    Immediate: Remove 5 mana tokens from this territory to draw 1 card.


Tactical Unit (cost 3):

Channeler (Strength: 3)
    Immediate: Place as many mana tokens on this territory as the number of meat and plants tokens both the neighbouring territories and itself could produce OR destroy a Crystal card from your hand to place 5 mana tokens on this territory. (mana tokens are discarded after the battle)


Champion Unit (cost 5):

Sorcerer (Strength: 4)
    Delayed: Remove 10 mana tokens from this territory to add 3 strength to this unit.


Immortal Unit (cost 6):

Spirit of Nature (Strength: 1)
    Permanent: If this unit should be destroyed when it is not doomed, doom it instead.
    Permanent: +3 strength for each mana token placed on this card.
    Reaction (mana tokens added to this territory): remove a mana token from this territory and place it on this card.
