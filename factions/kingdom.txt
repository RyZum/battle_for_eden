Valuable Resource: Ore

Basic Unit (cost 1):

Infantry (Strength: 1)
    Immediate: Reveal the top card of your deck. If it is a unit of strength 1, add it to the battle on this front. Otherwise discard it.


Shock Unit (cost 2):

Spearman (Strength: 1)
    Immediate: Discard a unit of strength 1 from your hand to destroy an enemy unit and draw a card.


Warrior Unit (cost 3):

Cavalry (Strength: 1)
    Immediate: Discard a unit of strength 1 from your hand to draw 2 cards, then discard 1 card from your hand.


Tactical Unit (cost 3):

Recruiter (Strength: 1)
    Immediate: Reveal the top two cards from your deck. Draw all units of strength 1 into your hand, discard any number of remaining cards and put the rest back on top of the deck in any order. Tactical Units cannot be drawn in this way. OR Destroy 1 card from your hand (there must be an opponent to choose this).


Champion Unit (cost 5):

Knight (Strength: 2)
    Permanent: +1 strength for each type of allied unit on this front


Immortal Unit (cost 6):

General (Strength: 5)
    Immediate: Trigger an allied unit's immediate ability.
