Civilizations rise and fall across the multiverse.
Meanwhile, a place is left untouched, never knowing magic or technology.
A land of abundance filled with all kinds of riches, all kinds of resources, even the rare magic crystals: a land called Eden!

When portals to Eden suddendly open throughout the multiverse, it is time for civilizations to go to war! War for crystals! War for resources!
This is the battle for Eden!
